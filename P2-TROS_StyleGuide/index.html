ABOUT TROS

Sustainability and Minimalism

Beyond a season, a trend, a moment, to a more sustainable world. Discover a destination dedicated to progress. We want to share the whole story; the beginning, middle and beyond of everything we do and make. From each new innovative material to the suppliers we work with. Beyond our collections, within our production chains and throughout our community. Because we all know that better fashion isn’t about the surface. It goes way beyond that...

What does the company do, or offer?


TROS - Tree + Eco, a Female sustainable fashion brand. TROS offers a distinctive design-led, minimalist style that felt modern and very different from the rest of our British wardrobes.


Who is the company to potential customers?


TROS is targeting those who design their lifestyle very simple and modern. Furthermore, as part of my primary research and curiosity, we spoke to one of TROS Store Managers, he explained that most of the customers are those who work in creative industry and communication such as fashion stylist, digital content creator. As location-wise, it is strategically in the centre of the business district, many young professionals aged 24 to 35 come to purchase their pieces in a more classic and smart style. They also have other customers older than 40 up to 60 who regularly come to the store to check and purchase their new collections.


Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service


TROS uses some eco-friendly materials including recycled materials and has fast fashion traits such as on trend styles and regular new arrivals. The TROS aesthetic is timeless design offered in a contemporary way with a focus on quality.
